SYNTHIA STOCK TICKER (2000)

LYNN HERSHMAN LEESON
PROGRAMMING BY: LIOR SAAR & COLIN KLINGMAN
15 x 11 1/4 x 11 1/4 IN
NETWORK-BASED MULTIMEDIA ARTWORK
the behavior of ‘synthia,’ the subject of the sculpture is determined by real-time stock market fluctuations. Synthia has sixteen predetermined actions based upon two percent changes in accumulated stock data (based on Dow Jones Industrial average, NASDAQ, S&P500 and the Russell Cap Indexes. when the market has an upward trend, synthia dances and shops at dior; when the market is down, she chain smokes, has nightmares and shops at Goodwill. synthia’s entire personality is determined by the state of the economy. leeson’s work depicts a women tied to the volatile stock market - a women is manipulated by an external force; synthia lacks autonomy. synthia exists in the context of cyberfeminism, a feminist response to the rise od the internet and virtual identity.
